About the Program

Africa's Premier AI Research Internship

At Nextar, interns are not observers — they are active contributors working on live products, research, and innovation that matters.

The Nextar Internship Program is a research-driven, innovation-focused experience designed for ambitious students and early-career professionals who want to work on real AI systems, software platforms, and high-impact technology solutions for Africa.

Interns work directly within active research pods, startup teams, and engineering projects — contributing to live products, publications, experiments, and innovation initiatives.

Collaboration happens in multidisciplinary teams under the mentorship of experienced engineers, researchers, and startup leads.

Program Structure

The Four-Phase Journey

A structured progression designed to maximise your growth — from day one through to your final showcase.

Phase 01
Onboarding & Orientation
  • Introduction to Nextar culture and systems
  • Team placement and research pod allocation
  • Technical environment setup
Phase 02
Intensive Learning & Collaboration
  • Guided technical tasks and workshops
  • Mentorship sessions with industry experts
  • Research and product contribution
  • Weekly evaluations and feedback
Phase 03
Project Execution
  • Research papers and AI prototypes
  • Software products and deployable applications
  • Startup solutions and live system contributions
Phase 04
Demo & Evaluation
  • Final project showcase and demo
  • Technical presentation to stakeholders
  • Performance review and certification
